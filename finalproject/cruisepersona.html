The Initial Plans for Fantasticruise.org

Site Purpose

This site's purpose is to provide cruise information for the people who want to enjoy outdoor activities and plan for such activities, such as waveriding or surfing.

Audience Description

The expected audience description for this site is made of people of the Middle Class to get a vacation. People will be tired of working every single day and will come to this site in order to arrange a cruise for recreation purposes.

User Persona

Mr. Heavy is a 34 year old man living in Salem, Oregan. With a well-paid job, He sits at a desk and crunches numbers and gets lots of monies in return for his services. However, once in a while, Mr. Heavy would like to take a rest. Therfore, he would use this site to schedule a cruise to rest and relax.
